What it is

What a Strategy Call Actually Is

It is not a sales call. I am not going to walk you through a deck and pitch you a package. It is a working conversation about where your business is, what you are trying to solve, and what the most direct path forward actually looks like.

If something genuinely good already exists and will do the job, I will tell you that. If the thing you think you need is not actually what you need, I will tell you that too. And if there is a real implementation path worth pursuing, we will sketch it out together before the call ends.
