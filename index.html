About

Your systems work.
They just cost too much of your time.

I am Simon — a Workflow Systems Architect who helps owner-operated businesses eliminate the manual bottlenecks holding them back.

Before this, I spent 20 years in construction. That background shaped how I think about systems: measure twice, build for durability, and never forget that someone has to actually live with what you create.

I work with legal practices, creative agencies, consultants, and e-commerce brands. The sweet spot? Businesses that have working systems but too much manual admin holding them together.

I do not resell SaaS wrappers. I build integrated systems you own — connecting tools that do not talk to each other, replacing copy-paste workflows, and creating infrastructure that scales without adding headcount.
